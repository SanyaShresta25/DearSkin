"How often should I exfoliate my combination skin?"

For combination skin, exfoliating 2-3 times per week is generally recommended. Start with once a week and gradually increase frequency based on how your skin responds.

Pay attention to how your skin feels after exfoliation - if it feels tight or irritated, reduce frequency.
